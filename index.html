Interesting Facts About BJJ

  • BJJ was developed from Judo in the early 20th century.
  • It focuses on ground fighting and submissions rather than striking.
  • The belt system in BJJ consists of White, Blue, Purple, Brown, and Black belts.
  • BJJ is often referred to as the "gentle art" because it emphasizes technique over brute strength.
  • Many MMA fighters have a strong BJJ background for grappling dominance.
